## Names to Avoid

- Anything with "Seer" (too close to SiteSeer conflict)
- Generic terms like "WebAudit" or "SiteChecker" (likely taken, not distinctive)
- Names that are hard to spell or pronounce
- Names that could have negative connotations in other languages
- Anything too similar to major competitors (Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, SEMrush, etc.)
