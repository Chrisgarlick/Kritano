The European Accessibility Act is coming. Are you ready?

What

EU-wide law requiring WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for digital products and services. Applies to any business operating in the EU market.

Who

E-commerce Banking Transport Telecoms Media All EU digital products

Non-Compliance

Financial penalties, service restrictions, and legal action — enforced at the national level by each EU member state.
