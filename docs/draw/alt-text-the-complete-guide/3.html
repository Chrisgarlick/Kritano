Why It Matters

Alt text isn't optional. It affects accessibility, SEO, and legal compliance.

A

Accessibility

285 million people worldwide are visually impaired. Alt text is how they experience your images.

S

SEO

Google can't "see" images. Alt text is how search engines understand and index your visual content.

L

Legal

WCAG 2.1, ADA, and the European Accessibility Act all require alt text. Non-compliance means fines.
