Good vs Bad Alt Text

Describe what the image communicates, not just what it contains.

Bad

alt="image"

alt="IMG_4392.jpg"

alt="photo of a dog sitting on grass in a park on a sunny day with trees"

Good

alt="Golden retriever sitting in a sunny park"

alt="Bar chart showing 40% increase in organic traffic"

Rule of thumb: Be specific, be brief (under 125 characters), and never start with "image of" or "photo of".
