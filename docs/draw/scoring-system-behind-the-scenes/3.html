Our Scoring Principles

Severity over quantity. 1 critical issue weighs more than 10 minor ones.
Unique issues, not total. 50 pages with the same broken link = 1 issue, not 50.
Fair across site sizes. A 20-page blog and a 10,000-page store use the same scale.
