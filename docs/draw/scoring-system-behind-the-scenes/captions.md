## Variation 2 — Dark with Code Block

**Caption:**
Should 1 critical issue weigh more than 10 minor ones?

We think so. A single exposed .env file is worse than 10 missing alt texts — even though the count is lower. That's why our scoring engine uses severity multipliers, not just issue counts.

Score of 92 with 0 critical issues vs 61 with 1 critical issue. The number tells you what to fix first.
