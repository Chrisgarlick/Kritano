Green on Dark

Success messages, status indicators, and badges on dark UIs. Green isn't always as readable as it looks.

Fail — AA 2.8:1

Payment successful. Your order is confirmed.

#10B981 on #0F172A
Pass — AA 7.3:1

Payment successful. Your order is confirmed.

#6EE7B7 on #0F172A

Tip: On dark backgrounds, use the 300 shade of green (emerald-300) instead of 500. It's lighter, passes WCAG, and still reads as "success".
