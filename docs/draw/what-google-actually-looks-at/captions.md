## Slide 2 — LCP (Largest Contentful Paint)

**Caption:**
LCP: Largest Contentful Paint.

How long until the biggest thing on screen shows up. Think of it as walking into a restaurant — LCP is how long before anyone acknowledges you. Over 2.5 seconds? You're losing visitors.

Quick win: compress and preload your hero image.

## Slide 3 — INP (Interaction to Next Paint)

**Caption:**
INP: Interaction to Next Paint.

How fast your site responds when someone taps a button. It's like pressing a lift button — even half a second of nothing feels broken. Google wants under 200ms.

Quick win: break up long JavaScript tasks and defer non-critical scripts.

## Slide 4 — CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift)

**Caption:**
CLS: Cumulative Layout Shift.

How much stuff jumps around while loading. You go to tap "Add to Cart" and the page shifts — now you've hit "Delete Account". Layout shift destroys trust.

Quick win: set explicit width and height on all images and embeds.
