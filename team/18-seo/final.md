# SEO Department - Final Output

## Executive Summary

This is Kritano's complete SEO strategy - covering keyword research, topic cluster architecture, on-page specifications, technical SEO requirements, AI/AEO visibility, and off-page link building. The strategy is built bottom-up: BOFU (revenue-driving) keywords first, then MOFU (comparison/evaluation), then TOFU (awareness).

Kritano has a strong technical foundation (robots.txt, meta tag infrastructure, some structured data) but is missing critical elements: no sitemap.xml, no pillar pages, no Article schema on blog posts, and no systematic internal linking. The 23 existing blog posts map cleanly into 5 topic clusters but lack the pillar pages that tie them together.

The single biggest opportunity is **Content Quality / AEO** - a category Kritano essentially owns. No competitor audits content quality the way Kritano does, and the keyword space ("content quality checker", "E-E-A-T checker", "answer engine optimisation checker") has almost zero competition. This is first-mover territory.

## Key Decisions & Recommendations

- **Priority 1:** Generate sitemap.xml immediately - robots.txt references it but it doesn't exist
- **Priority 2:** Create the 5 pillar pages (one per topic cluster) - these are the backbone of the content strategy
- **Priority 3:** Implement Article schema + FAQPage schema on all blog posts
- **Priority 4:** Build out the "Content Quality" cluster - this is Kritano's unique differentiator
- **Priority 5:** Launch on Product Hunt + list on G2/Capterra for entity signals
- **Money keywords to own:** "website audit tool", "accessibility checker", "WCAG compliance checker", "website health check tool", "EAA compliance checker"
- **Content Quality keywords are uncontested:** "content quality checker", "E-E-A-T checker tool", "answer engine optimisation checker" - publish now before competitors notice
- **Bottom-up approach:** Prioritise BOFU and MOFU content over TOFU. Revenue-driving content first.
- **AI visibility is a channel:** Structure all content for AI extraction (concise answers, FAQ sections, named authors, deep topic clusters)
