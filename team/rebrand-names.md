# Kritano Rebrand: Name Candidates

**Prepared:** 26 March 2026 | **Updated:** 26 March 2026 (Round 2)
**Purpose:** Vetted name candidates for potential rebrand, scored by domain availability, competitive conflict, brand fit, and overall viability.

---

## Why Rebrand?

- "PagePulse" (one letter different) has 5+ active products including a NZ web monitoring company (since 2016)
- "Pulse" is crowded in the accessibility space: WCAG Pulse, A11y Pulse are direct competitors
- "PagePulser" and "PagePulse" were nearly indistinguishable when spoken
- SEO for own brand name returned PagePulse results, not PagePulser
- **DECISION: Rebranded to Kritano (27 March 2026)**

## Naming Criteria

**Hard requirements:**
- .com domain available or acquirable
- No existing companies in web auditing/accessibility/SEO tooling
- Pronounceable in English, French, German, Spanish
- Spellable after hearing it once
- Under 12 characters

**Desirable:**
- Works as both noun and verb
- Implies thoroughness/depth, not speed
- Avoids: "pulse", "scan", "check", "bot", "AI"
- Suggests ongoing monitoring (subscription model)
- Does not box the product into a single audit category

## Confidence Scoring

Each name is rated on a 1-10 scale across five dimensions:

| Dimension | What It Measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Domain** | .com availability and path to acquisition (10 = clearly available, 1 = taken by major company) |
| **Conflict** | Existing companies/products with same name (10 = no conflicts, 1 = direct competitor) |
| **Brand Fit** | How well the name communicates what Kritano does (10 = perfect match, 1 = no connection) |
| **Memorability** | How easy it is to remember, spell, and say aloud (10 = instantly memorable, 1 = forgettable) |
| **Global** | Cross-lingual pronunciation and no negative meanings (10 = works everywhere, 1 = problematic) |
| **Overall** | Weighted average: Domain (25%) + Conflict (25%) + Brand Fit (20%) + Memorability (15%) + Global (15%) |

## Recommended Next Steps

1. **Domain check the top 5** — Use instantdomainsearch.com or Namecheap to verify .com availability for: scrutin, siteproof, clarivo, verdikt, pagesight
2. **Trademark search** — Run the top 5 through UKIPO (trademarks.ipo.gov.uk) and EUIPO (euipo.europa.eu/eSearch) for Classes 9 and 42

4. **Shortlist to 3** — Based on domain/trademark results, narrow to 3 finalists
5. **User testing** — Share the 3 finalists with 5-10 people in the target audience (freelancers, agency owners) and ask: "What do you think this product does?" and "Can you spell it after hearing it once?"
