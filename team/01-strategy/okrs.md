# Vision, Mission, Values & OKRs — PagePulser

## Vision

A web where every website is fast, accessible, secure, and written for humans — because the tools to get there are available to everyone, not just enterprises with six-figure budgets.

## Mission

Give website owners — from solo founders to 50-person agencies — a single platform that diagnoses what's wrong with their site, explains why it matters, and shows them exactly how to fix it. Replace the fragmented workflow of 3-4 separate tools with one scan, one dashboard, one source of truth.

## Values

### 1. Clarity Over Completeness
A prioritised list of 10 fixes in plain English beats a 400-line CSV of raw findings. Every output should answer "so what?" and "now what?" — not just "what."

### 2. Accessible to Everyone (Including Us)
We audit accessibility. We must practise it. Our own interface, pricing, and documentation should be usable by the widest possible audience. Free tier exists because access matters, not just as a funnel.

### 3. Honest Diagnosis
We don't inflate scores to make users feel good, and we don't manufacture urgency to drive upgrades. If a site scores 90, we say so. If a finding is minor, we label it minor. Trust is the product.

### 4. Depth, Not Breadth-For-Marketing
Every check we ship must be genuinely useful — not padding to make a feature comparison table look longer. Fewer, better checks beat hundreds of trivial ones.

### 5. Ship, Then Iterate
A working feature with rough edges beats a perfect spec in a document. The founding members are signing up to help build this — ship early, listen fast, improve continuously.

## OKRs — Founding Member Launch (Q2 2026)

### Objective 1: Validate product-market fit with founding members
- **KR1:** 100 of 200 founding member spots claimed within 30 days of launch
- **KR2:** 60% of founding members complete their first audit within 48 hours of registration
- **KR3:** 30% of founding members run 3+ audits in their first month (activation)
- **KR4:** NPS score ≥ 40 from founding member survey at day 30

### Objective 2: Demonstrate conversion from free to paid
- **KR1:** 15% of founding members convert from trial to paid subscription before trial ends (day 30)
- **KR2:** 40% of paid conversions choose annual billing
- **KR3:** Average revenue per paying user ≥ $45/month (weighted toward Pro/Agency tiers)
- **KR4:** Zero involuntary churn from billing issues (Stripe webhook handling works cleanly)

### Objective 3: Establish content intelligence as the positioning differentiator
- **KR1:** Content Quality Score is the #1 cited feature in founding member feedback
- **KR2:** 3 organic blog posts/social mentions reference PagePulser's content analysis specifically
- **KR3:** E-E-A-T and AEO check categories have ≥ 70% usage rate among Pro+ users
- **KR4:** At least 1 agency founding member uses content scores in client-facing reports

### Objective 4: Build a sustainable acquisition engine
- **KR1:** 20+ organic signups per month from blog content (UTM-tracked)
- **KR2:** Referral program generates 30+ referred signups in first 60 days
- **KR3:** Cold prospect pipeline surfaces 50+ qualified leads per week
- **KR4:** Public audit badges appear on ≥ 10 external websites within 60 days of launch

## OKRs — Post-Launch Growth (Q3 2026)

### Objective 5: Scale to 500 active users
- **KR1:** 500 registered users with ≥ 1 completed audit
- **KR2:** 50 paying customers (10% conversion from free/trial)
- **KR3:** MRR reaches £2,500
- **KR4:** Weekly active usage (≥ 1 login/week) ≥ 50% of paying users

### Objective 6: Ship the differentiating features
- **KR1:** Template-based fix snippets live for top 50 findings
- **KR2:** EAA Compliance Passport shipped and generating reports
- **KR3:** Agency Portfolio Dashboard shipped with ≥ 5 agency users actively using it
- **KR4:** Webhook management UI completed, ≥ 3 users with active webhooks
