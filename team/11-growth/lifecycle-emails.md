# Lifecycle Email Sequences -- Kritano

---

## Sequence 1: Welcome / Onboarding

**Trigger:** User registers
**Goal:** First completed audit within 24 hours

| Timing | Subject | Content Summary | CTA |
|--------|---------|-----------------|-----|
| Immediate | Welcome to Kritano -- run your first audit in 60 seconds | Quick-start steps: add site, click audit, see results. Include direct link to dashboard. | "Run Your First Audit" |
| +24h (if no audit) | Your website audit is waiting | Remind them why they signed up. Show what they'll learn (scores across 6 pillars). | "Add Your First Site" |
| +72h (if no audit) | Most sites score between 40 and 60 -- where's yours? | Curiosity hook. Mention it takes under 2 minutes. Social proof: "X audits completed this week." | "Find Out Your Score" |
| +7d (if no audit) | Quick question | Simple text email: "Hey, I noticed you signed up but haven't run an audit yet. Was something confusing? I'd love to help." Signed from Chris personally. | Reply |

## Sequence 2: Post-First-Audit

**Trigger:** First audit completed
**Goal:** Drive understanding of results and return visit

| Timing | Subject | Content Summary | CTA |
|--------|---------|-----------------|-----|
| +1h | Here's what your scores mean | Explain each pillar score briefly. Link to their specific results. Highlight their best and worst scores. | "View Full Results" |
| +3d | 3 quick wins from your audit | Pull their top 3 easiest-to-fix issues. Explain each in plain English with the fix. | "View Your Issues" |
| +7d | Your website vs the average | Compare their scores to anonymised averages. "Your accessibility score is 52 -- the average site we audit scores 48." | "Improve Your Score" |
| +14d | Time for a re-audit? | "It's been 2 weeks since your first audit. If you've made any changes, re-audit to see your progress." | "Re-Run Audit" |

## Sequence 3: Trial Expiry (Early Access Users)

**Trigger:** Agency trial approaching expiry
**Goal:** Convert to paid

| Timing | Subject | Content Summary | CTA |
|--------|---------|-----------------|-----|
| -7d | Your Agency trial ends in 7 days | Recap what they've used: X audits, X issues found, X sites monitored. "Here's what stays and what changes." | "View Plans" |
| -3d | 3 days left on your Agency trial | Feature comparison: what they keep (free tier) vs what they lose. Emphasise the features they've actually used. | "Upgrade Now" |
| -1d | Last day of your Agency trial | Direct, honest: "Your trial ends tomorrow. If Kritano has been useful, here's how to keep everything." | "Choose Your Plan" |
| 0 | Your trial has ended | Clear summary of what's now locked. "Your data is safe -- upgrade anytime to unlock it." No pressure. | "Upgrade" |
| +3d | We kept your data safe | Reassurance + gentle nudge. "Everything you've built is still there. Pick up where you left off." | "View Your Dashboard" |
| +7d | Quick question | Plain text from Chris: "What held you back from upgrading? Genuinely curious -- your feedback shapes what we build." | Reply |

## Sequence 4: Score Improvement Celebration

**Trigger:** User's score improves by 5+ points on any pillar
**Goal:** Positive reinforcement + viral sharing

| Timing | Subject | Content Summary | CTA |
|--------|---------|-----------------|-----|
| Immediate | Your [pillar] score just improved by [X] points! | Celebrate the improvement. Show before/after. "Here's what changed." | "View Your Progress" |
| (Same email) | Share your progress | Referral link + pre-written social share messages. "Know someone who'd find this useful?" | "Share" / "Invite a Friend" |

## Sequence 5: Win-Back (Churned Users)

**Trigger:** 60+ days inactive
**Goal:** Re-engage or learn why they left

| Timing | Subject | Content Summary | CTA |
|--------|---------|-----------------|-----|
| Day 60 | A lot has changed since you last visited | Highlight 2-3 new features or improvements shipped since they were last active. | "See What's New" |
| Day 75 | One free premium audit, on us | Offer a single free audit at their previous tier level. No strings. | "Run Free Audit" |
| Day 90 | We'd love your honest feedback | Final email. Short survey (3 questions max) or reply-to asking what didn't work. | "Share Feedback" |

## Email Design Principles

1. **Plain text style.** HTML emails that look like personal emails outperform designed newsletters for SaaS.
2. **Short.** Under 150 words for transactional, under 300 for marketing.
3. **One CTA per email.** Never compete with yourself.
4. **From Chris, not Kritano.** Personal sender name builds trust.
5. **British English.** Always.
6. **No fake urgency.** "Your trial ends in 3 days" is a fact. "ACT NOW!" is not our voice.
7. **Unsubscribe in every email.** Legal requirement and trust signal.
